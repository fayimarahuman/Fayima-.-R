PK RESTAURANT

Most of you might be wondering what Pk Restaurant is !!! . But i have all answers for your questions.

PK stands for Peter Kagoroti. This Restaurant was started by a Kenyan man and his wife Purity Kagoroti. It is located in Entebbe town next to Entebbe travellers hotel.They mainly deal in selling of goats meat mainly roasted, fish, and chicken.

Note : All food types are sold in form of wet fry , dry fry , roasted .
